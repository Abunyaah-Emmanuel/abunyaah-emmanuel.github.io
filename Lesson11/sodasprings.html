The atmospheric condition in Soda Springs, Idaho

By Emmanuel Abunyaah

SODA SPRINGS, Idaho gets 16 inches of rain, on average, per year. The US average is 38 inches of rain per year. Soda Springs averages 72 inches of snow per year. The US average is 28 inches of snow per year. On average, there are 200 sunny days per year in Soda Springs. The US average is 205 sunny days.

Soda Springs gets some kind of precipitation, on average, 106 days per year. Precipitation is rain, snow, sleet, or hail that falls to the ground. In order for precipitation to be counted you have to get at least .01 inches on the ground to measure. Here's the Soda Springs Weather Highlights.
