Target Audience

Target Audience

The target audience of the Ghana Meteorological Agency web site is described below:

  • The Government of Ghana: This is comprised of regional and national Government Agencies, emergency services and ministries. The regional and national media, Government-sector enterprise (telecommunications, banking, mining, etc.), universities, and both national and regional coorporate entities.
  • Local Communities: This is comprised of community leaders, farm cooperative leaders, village leadership, regional politicians, students, teachers, community members that don’t work in farming but occupy positions of respect, local NGOs, extension services and medium-scale local enterprisers.
  • Agriculturalists: This large, diverse target group consists of crop farmers, small or large scale farmers, industrialized farmers, fishermen and everyone involved in the agricultural value chain, ranging from seed salesmen to livestock buyers.
  • Private Sector: Private sector enterprises benefit from tailored weather information – to protect human and physical resources and make climate-smart business decisions – they can also play a role in disseminating emergency messages.
