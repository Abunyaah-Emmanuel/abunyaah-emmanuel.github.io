Navigation

Navigation

Navigation will sit near the top of the page, between the header and the main elements. It will have the following characteristics:

  • Unselected item: It will be Olive Dark Green (#556b2f) background with White (#ffffff) for the text.
  • Hovered Item: Color scheme, with Space Cadet (#1f2041) background with White (#ffffff) for the text.
  • Selected Item: It will be Olive Dark Green (#556b2f) background with Orange (#ffa500) for the text.
