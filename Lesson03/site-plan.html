Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Meteorology Research Institute website purpose is to provide the general public and stake holders of the government of Ghana the neccesary meteorolical information needed, its purpose and intended audience, utilization scenarios, and other aspects such as its organization and structure including elements of style will forsee, for generations to come, the developement of the meteorological Agency in Ghana.

Site Name

This site is going to be called Meteorology Research Institute of Ghana (MRIGHANA). As the name indicates, it will provide efficient and reliable meteorological information by collecting, processing, achiving, and analyzing of meteorolical information to clients.

Company Logo

Purpose Statement

The main purpose of the website will be to provide timely, accurate, and cost-effective weather and climate services by collecting, processing, achiving, and analyzing of meteorological information to support socio-economic development in Ghana. This establishment will be equiped with High-Advanced Technology that will provide acurate meteorological information. A secondary purpose is to provide a timely weather information to aids through weather forecasts and warnings.
